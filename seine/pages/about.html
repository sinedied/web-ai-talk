Our Story

Founded in 2020, Seine started with a simple mission: make premium tech products available to everyone. From our headquarters along the banks of the Seine river in Paris, we curate and design products that combine cutting-edge technology with modern aesthetics.

Today, our team of engineers and designers continues to push boundaries — from headphones and smart devices to accessories and gadgets — bringing the best tech to everyday life.
